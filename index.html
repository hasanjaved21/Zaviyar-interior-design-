About Zaviyar Interior Design

Founded in 2015, Zaviyar Interior Design began with a simple vision: to make luxury interior design accessible without compromising on quality. What started as a small studio in Karachi has grown into a trusted name, known for transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every space tells a story. We combine artistic vision with practical functionality, ensuring each project reflects the unique personality of its inhabitants. From cozy apartments to grand villas, our team pours creativity and precision into every detail.

With over 128 successful projects and 73+ delighted clients, we continue to push boundaries, blending contemporary elegance with timeless sophistication.
